What is PPHC all about?

They assist the children who have no shelter, no food, no parental guidance.
They are an orphanage that keep in orphans that are in need of mental and physical
assistance. They have different clients and residents.
They provide Psychosocial Intervention, Medical services and educational services.
This institution is located in Rambutan St., Tugbok Proper, Davao City.
